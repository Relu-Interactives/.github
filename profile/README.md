## 👋 Who we are

**Relu Interactives** is a Ghana-based immersive technology studio. We build interactive 3D
software, AR/VR experiences, and the spatial web — turning real-world spaces, training, learning,
and play into something you can step inside. From the browser to the headset, we make immersive
tech accessible to everyone.

## ✦ What we build

| | Focus area | What it means |
|---|---|---|
| 🌐 | **WebXR & the Spatial Web** | AR/VR that runs in any browser — no app, no install |
| 🎮 | **Games & Simulations** | Immersive games and realistic training simulations |
| 🏗️ | **Digital Twins & Engineering Viz** | Interactive 3D models of real-world assets and sites |
| 📚 | **Immersive Learning** | Educational experiences that transport learners anywhere |
| 🤝 | **Enterprise & Collaboration** | 3D tools that boost engagement and productivity |

## 🚀 Featured product — Relu Spatial

> ### **VR & AR for everyone**

[**Relu Spatial**](https://reluspatial.com) brings augmented and virtual reality straight to the
web browser — no dedicated app, no headset required. It removes the barriers to immersive tech so
anyone can create, share, and explore spatial experiences with nothing more than a link.
