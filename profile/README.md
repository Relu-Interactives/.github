## 🌍 Who we are

**Relu Interactives** is a Ghana-based immersive-technology studio. We design and build interactive
3D software, AR/VR experiences, and the spatial web — turning real-world spaces, training, learning,
and play into something you can step inside. From the browser to the headset, we make immersive tech
accessible to everyone, everywhere.

## ✨ Our mission

> **Building Africa's future through interactive technology.**

We believe the next generation of software is spatial. Our mission is to put immersive,
interactive experiences in the hands of businesses, educators, and creators across the
continent — and to prove that world-class XR can be built in Africa, for the world.

## 🚀 What we do

### 🌐 WebXR & the Spatial Web
AR and VR that run in any browser — no app, no install, no headset required. We build the spatial
web so an experience is only ever a link away.

### 🎮 Games & Simulations
Immersive games and high-fidelity training simulations that engage, teach, and prepare people for
the real thing in a safe, repeatable environment.

### 🏗️ Digital Twins & Engineering Visualization
Interactive, real-time 3D replicas of real-world assets, sites, and systems — for monitoring,
planning, and decision-making.

### 📚 Immersive Learning
Educational experiences that transport learners anywhere, making complex ideas tangible through
interaction instead of memorization.

## ⭐ Featured product — Relu Spatial

[**Relu Spatial**](https://reluspatial.com) brings augmented and virtual reality straight to the web
browser — **VR & AR for everyone**, with no dedicated app and no headset required. It removes the
barriers to immersive tech so anyone can create, share, and explore spatial experiences with nothing
more than a link.
